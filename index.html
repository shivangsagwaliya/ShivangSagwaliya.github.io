Tech Solutions Corp Sales & Supply Chain Exploratory Data Analysis | Python

Analyzed a complex dataset of 192,921 records across 5 distinct data sources (Orders, Customers, Employees, Products, Suppliers) to evaluate business performance. The project focused on data integrity, feature engineering, and extracting actionable insights.

  • Financial Health: Validated overall profitability of $356.66M, with high-value transactions showing a strong correlation (0.86) to profit.
  • Sector Performance: Healthcare ($241M) and Telecommunications emerged as the strongest industries.
  • Logistics Efficiency: Calculated an average fulfillment timeline of 8.82 days, with a low return rate of 2% and cancellation rate of 3%, indicating a highly efficient supply chain.
  • Geographic Dominance: Identified New York as the primary revenue driver (>$205M sales), while flagging Ohio and Michigan as critical underperformers requiring strategic intervention.
Tech Solutions Corp Sales & Operations Intelligence Dashboard | Power BI, DAX

Designed and developed a comprehensive executive dashboard for "Tech Solutions Corp" to visualize end-to-end business performance. The analysis covers 193,000 orders generating $2.39 billion in revenue, providing stakeholders with actionable insights into sales trends, employee productivity, and supply chain efficiency.

  • Sales & Product Strategy:
    • Software Licenses are the highest-performing category ($29M), significantly outperforming hardware like Keyboards ($2M).
    • Software Licenses offer the highest Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) at 63K.
    • New York office contributes 39.16% of total revenue
  • Workforce Efficiency: Evaluated performance for 150 employees with an average revenue per employee of $15.94M.
  • Operational Health: Monitored supply chain logistics, revealing an average Order Fulfillment Time of 8.8 days (3 days processing + 5.8 days delivery), cancellation rate of 3% and a return rate of 2%, identifying Office Chairs as the product with the highest cancellation volume.
